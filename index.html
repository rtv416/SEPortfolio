DeGlyph

Independently developing a web-based glyph decoding platform inspired by the TV series Fringe, where glyphs are mysterious symbols used to encode hidden messages between episodes. Due to a slight obsession with the show, I wanted to create a simple way to decode these glyphs and store them to uncover their hidden meanings throughout the series. DeGlyph features secure user authentication and data storage powered by Firebase, enabling users to log in and save decoded words across episodes. The platform also includes a guest mode for quick, login-free decoding. Designed with scalability and user accessibility in mind, the project is planned for deployment on the Google Play Store upon completion.
